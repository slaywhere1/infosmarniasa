If your access code has not arrived

Please make sure that your device:

  • is turned on, not in flight mode and has a strong signal
  • can receive text messages from other senders

If you have made these checks and are still not receiving text messages from us then restart your device.

You will not be able to add or change how you get your access codes until this expires.
